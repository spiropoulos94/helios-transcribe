**Note**: ElevenLabs Scribe v2 offers the best Greek accuracy for proper nouns, while Gemini 3 Pro Preview is more cost-effective for general Greek transcription.

## Recommendations

- **For best value**: Use Gemini 3 Pro Preview ($0.44/hour)
- **For best Greek accuracy**: Use ElevenLabs Scribe with Professional plan ($0.50/hour)
- **For occasional use**: Starter plan is acceptable at $0.54/hour
